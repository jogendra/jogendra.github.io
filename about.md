Hi, I’m Jogendra.

Originally from India 🇮🇳 and now based in Amsterdam, Netherlands 🇳🇱

I’m a software engineer working across backend systems and iOS, with a strong interest in building thoughtful, reliable products that combine solid engineering with great user experience.

Outside of tech, aviation has become a big part of my life. What started as a fascination with flying during the COVID years eventually turned into something much more serious — I’m now an EASA Private Pilot License (PPL-A) holder and regularly fly out of the Netherlands. Long term, I’d love to build meaningful technology in the aviation space and contribute to the future of flying in my own way.

I graduated in 2020 with Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Engineering Physics from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) Varanasi.

When I’m away from my laptop or the cockpit, you’ll probably find me riding motorcycles, playing football, cricket or tennis, traveling, or learning something new just because it sounds interesting.

This space is a reflection of the things I genuinely care about.
